# Character Creation

## Building Your Character

Follow these steps to create your High Societea character.

### Step 1: Basic Information

Fill in your character's basic details:
- **Name** - Your character's name
- **Title** - Their social title (Duke, Duchess, Lady, Lord, etc.)
- **Background** - A brief description of their history
- **Personality Traits** - Key characteristics

### Step 2: Ability Scores

Determine your six ability scores. You can either:
- Use the point-buy system (27 points to distribute)
- Roll 4d6, drop the lowest die, repeat 6 times
- Use the standard array: 15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8

### Step 3: Skills

Choose skills that fit your character concept. Each skill has a rank from -2 to +5 or higher.

Common skills include:
- **Persuasion** - Convince others of your viewpoint
- **Deception** - Hide the truth or tell lies
- **Insight** - Read people's intentions
- **Etiquette** - Navigate social situations
- **Tea Knowledge** - Understand tea varieties and preparation

### Step 4: Equipment & Inventory

Choose starting equipment that reflects your character's background and social standing.

### Step 5: Portrait

Add a portrait to bring your character to life! You can upload an image or use a URL.

## Character Development

As you play, your character will grow and develop. Keep track of:
- Experience points
- New skills learned
- Items acquired
- Relationships formed
